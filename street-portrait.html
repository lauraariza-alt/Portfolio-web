Street Portrait

If you are interested in this type of photography, you should know about it, and with this genre of photography you are able to record everyday life in public places, it´s most of the time taking pictures of strangers, usually without them knowing. Personally I don´t have a social purpose when I do Street portraits, I prefer to go out, set apart and capture moments, in which I think I go unnoticed.
